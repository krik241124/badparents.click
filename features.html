Game Features

Core Gameplay

Psychological Horror

  • Deep exploration of childhood trauma and family dynamics

  • Focus on psychological tension over jump scares

Narrative Experience

  • Compact 20-30 minute storyline
  • Single, focused narrative path
  • Deep emotional impact
  • Carefully crafted character interactions
