Frequently Asked Questions

About the Game

What is Bad Parenting?

Bad Parenting is a narrative-driven psychological horror game that explores themes of family dynamics and childhood trauma. Players follow the story of Ron as he investigates the mysterious Mr. Red Face and uncovers disturbing truths about his family.

What platforms is Bad Parenting available on?

Bad Parenting is currently available for PC through Steam and other major gaming platforms.

How long does it take to complete Bad Parenting?

The game offers a compact but impactful experience, typically taking 20-30 minutes to complete. This focused duration allows for an intense and memorable narrative experience.

Is Bad Parenting based on a true story?

While Bad Parenting draws inspiration from real-world issues surrounding family dynamics and childhood experiences, the specific story and characters are fictional.

Gameplay and Features

What type of gameplay can I expect?

Bad Parenting focuses on exploration and narrative elements. Players will investigate their surroundings, uncover story elements, and experience psychological horror through atmospheric gameplay rather than combat.

Are there multiple endings?

The game follows a single, focused narrative path to deliver a specific and impactful story about Ron's experiences with Mr. Red Face.

What makes Bad Parenting unique?

The game stands out through its psychological horror elements, unique 90s cartoon-inspired visual style, and its exploration of serious themes related to family dynamics and childhood experiences.

Technical Information

What are the system requirements?

Bad Parenting uses a classic 3D style that runs well on most modern computers. The game has been optimized to provide a smooth experience on various PC configurations.

Does the game support different languages?

Yes, Bad Parenting supports multiple languages including English and Chinese, making it accessible to a global audience.

Can I save my progress?

The game is designed as a single-session experience and doesn't include save functionality, encouraging players to complete it in one sitting.

Content Warning

Is Bad Parenting suitable for all ages?

No, Bad Parenting contains mature themes and psychological horror elements. The game is intended for mature audiences only and may not be suitable for sensitive viewers.

What type of content should I be aware of?

The game deals with themes of family dynamics, psychological horror, and contains disturbing imagery. Players should be prepared for an intense emotional experience.

Support and Community

Where can I get help if I'm stuck?

You can find help through our community forums, Steam discussions, or by contacting our support team at support@badparents.click.

Can I share gameplay videos or streams?

Yes! We encourage players to share their Bad Parenting experience through videos, streams, and social media, as long as they follow platform guidelines and include appropriate content warnings.

Characters and Story

Who is Mr. Red Face?

Mr. Red Face is the central antagonist, initially presented as a fictional character created to teach children about behavior. His true nature reveals a darker manifestation of parental control and fear.

Who is Ron?

Ron is the main protagonist, a young boy who must confront his fears and navigate through disturbing encounters with Mr. Red Face while uncovering dark family secrets.

What role do Ron's parents play?

Bruce (father) and Robin (mother) are key characters whose actions and relationships with Ron form the foundation of the game's narrative about family dynamics and childhood trauma.

Gameplay Mechanics

How do I control the character?

The game uses simple, intuitive controls for movement and interaction. Use WASD or arrow keys to move, mouse to look around, and E or left-click to interact with objects.

Are there any combat elements?

No, Bad Parenting focuses on exploration and narrative rather than combat. The horror comes from psychological elements and atmosphere rather than direct confrontation.

What should I do if I encounter Mr. Red Face?

Each encounter with Mr. Red Face is scripted and part of the narrative. Follow the story prompts and environmental cues to progress through these encounters.

Game Progress

Are there checkpoints in the game?

Due to the short duration of the game (20-30 minutes), there are no checkpoints. The game is designed to be completed in one sitting for maximum impact.

Can I replay specific scenes?

The game doesn't feature a scene selection system. To experience specific scenes again, you'll need to start a new playthrough.

Is there a New Game+ mode?

No, Bad Parenting is designed as a single, impactful narrative experience without additional gameplay modes.

Technical Support

How do I report bugs?

Bugs can be reported through our Steam community page or by emailing our support team at support@badparents.click.

What should I do if the game crashes?

First, verify your game files through Steam. If issues persist, check our troubleshooting guide on the Steam community hub or contact support.

Does the game support modding?

No, Bad Parenting does not officially support modding to maintain the intended narrative experience.

Additional Features

Are there achievements?

No, the game focuses on delivering a concentrated narrative experience without achievement systems.

Is there a photo mode?

The game does not include a photo mode, but players can use Steam's screenshot feature (F12 by default) to capture moments.

Are there difficulty settings?

Bad Parenting offers a single difficulty level designed to provide the intended narrative experience for all players.

Future Content

Will there be a sequel?

There are currently no announced plans for a sequel. Bad Parenting is designed as a complete, self-contained story.

Are there plans for additional content?

The game is complete as released, with no current plans for DLC or additional content.

Will there be console versions?

Currently, Bad Parenting is PC-exclusive with no announced plans for console releases.
